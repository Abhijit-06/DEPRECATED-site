Thanks to some hard work by Jory Schossau and others, we have developed three standard questionnaires for bootcamp participants:

  • Pre-assessment for Learners
  • Post-assessment for Learners
  • Post-assessment for Instructors

We hope to deploy these questionnaires starting in September, so we would be very grateful for feedback: do you feel you understand what we're asking, do the responses provided include the answer you would give, and anything else you can think of.
