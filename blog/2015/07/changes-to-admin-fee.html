After discussion with our Advisory Council and Data Carpentry, we have agreed to make some changes to the administration fee we charge for workshops that we help organize:

  1. If a host site arranges instructors and handles registration, they do not owe us an administration fee.
  2. If we are involved in getting instructors or managing registration, the host owes us $2500 per workshop if they are not a partner or affiliate, and $1250 per workshop if they are. This price increase matches Data Carpentry's, which recently decided to the same change in their administrative fees.
  3. If a partner or affiliate recruits instructors and handles registration for another host, without using either SCF staff time or any staff time they might owe SCF, then they may charge what they like for the service (including nothing). If they do charge a fee, we strongly encourage them to make it similar to ours.
  4. We will continue to be generous with waivers for organizations that would otherwise not be able to afford them.

The new administrative fees will apply to all workshops whose dates are finalized after September 1, 2015. (Note that the transition is based on when the dates for the workshop are finalized, not when the workshop itself takes place.) If you have any questions, please get in touch.
