And this year edition of Google Summer of Code (GSoC) finished. We are very happy with the outcome of the work of the three students that worked under the NumFOCUS umbrella that we helped to coordinate.

The projects were:

  • Cython API for DyND,
  • Enhance Amy, a workshop-management platform for Software Carpentry,
  • JuliaQuantum: Framework for solvers.

And we are grateful for the great done by the mentors of the projects:

  • Alexander Croy,
  • Greg Wilson,
  • Irwin Zaid,
  • Mark Wiebe and
  • W. Trevor King.

Also, we are grateful for those that helped organizing GSoC, specially,

  • Carol Willing,
  • Ethan White,
  • Jason K. Moore and
  • Sheila Miguez.

We hope that the students continue involved with the communities they worked with during the summer and spreading the free/open source software. See you all again next year.
