--- layout: blog root: ../../.. author: Raniere Silva title: "NumFOCUS accepted as Google Summer of Code mentoring organization" date: 2015-03-03 time: "18:00:00" category: ["Community"] ---

A few weeks ago we announced that we will help NumFOCUS to apply to Google Summer of Code (GSoC) as a mentoring organization. Yesterday the list of mentoring organization was announced and NumFOCUS was selected.

I want to thanks very much (in alphabetical order) to Andy Terrel, Ethan White, Matt Sottile and W. Trevor King that volunteer to be mentors and make this possible.

We have a list of ideas here but students can apply with their own idea and in this case we suggest to follow this instructions so we could help polish the ideas. Application on any project supported by NumFOCUS are welcome.

Important dates:

16 March: 19:00 UTC Student application period opens.
27 March: 19:00 UTC Student application deadline.
