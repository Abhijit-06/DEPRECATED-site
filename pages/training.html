Learn

We run dozens of workshops a year at sites all over the world. If you'd like to hear about workshops near you, please subscribe to our our blog or join our announcements mailing list, or mail us directly.

Host

If you would like us to run a workshop at your university, research lab, company, or conference, the FAQ and Operations Guide have more information. You can also request a workshop in your area, and we're always happy to chat by email.

Note: our name and logo are are trademarked, and may not be used without our explicit prior permission. Please see our FAQ for details.

Teach

All of our slides, notes, and example programs are in our Git repositories under open access licenses: you are free to use them however you want so long as you cite us as the source. Everything is designed so that individuals can work through what they need on their own, but universities, research labs, companies, and self-study groups have also used it in week-long crash courses and as part of longer classroom courses.
