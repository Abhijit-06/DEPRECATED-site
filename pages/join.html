Discussion

You can join the conversation by following our blog or {{site.twitter_name}} on Twitter, or talk to us on these mailing lists:

announcements (archives) Announcements about upcoming workshops and other events. 3-5 messages/month moderated
instructors (archives) Advertisements for workshop instructors and helpers. 1-2 messages/month moderated
discuss (archives) The main channel for discussion of direction and technology. 5-20 messages/week unmoderated
matlab-discuss (archives) A sub-list for discussion of MATLAB materials and teaching. 1-5 messages/week unmoderated
r-discuss (archives) A sub-list for discussion of R materials and teaching. 5-20 messages/week unmoderated
oz (archives) A sub-list for discussion of activities in Australia and New Zealand. 5-20 messages/week unmoderated

Instructor Training

We run an online training course to help people become instructors. The online version requires 2-4 hours/week for 12 weeks, while the live version runs over two full days. Both cover the basics of educational psychology, instructional design, and how to apply both to teaching programming to adults. If you'd like to take part, please fill in this form and someone will get in touch within the week.
