Damien Irving is a PhD candidate in Meteorology at the University of Melbourne, where his research focuses on the climate of the high southern latitudes. He also writes a [blog](http://drclimate.wordpress.com/) about research best practice in the weather/climate sciences, teaches software skills to researchers through an innovative new project called the [Research Bazaar](http://melbourne.resbaz.edu.au/about), and is the unofficial coordinator of Software Carpentry activities in Australia.
