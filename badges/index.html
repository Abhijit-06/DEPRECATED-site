The web provides many new ways to gain skills and experience, including online courses, learning networks, mentorship, and peer learning. Digital badges provide an open, portable, verifiable way for learners to get recognition for what they know. Using Mozilla's Open Badge Infrastructure, Software Carpentry is issuing kinds of badges for:

  • creators who are producing teaching materials,
  • instructors who are qualified to teach our bootcamps, and
  • the organizers who make those bootcamps possible.

You can learn more about open badges here, or download our issuer information.
